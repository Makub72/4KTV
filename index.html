Choosing a Wall Mount

Mounting Specifications

  • VESA Pattern: 300 x 300 mm. The mounting holes form a 300mm square.
  • TV Weight: 47.4 lbs (21.5 kg) without the stand.
  • Required Capacity: Your mount must support at least 50 lbs.
  • Screw Size: Requires M6 screws (included with most mounts).

Types of Mounts

Fixed Mount

Sits closest to the wall for a low-profile look but doesn't move.

Tilting Mount

Angles up or down to reduce glare from lights or windows.

Full-Motion Mount

Extends, swivels, and tilts for maximum viewing flexibility.
